# Financial AI Assistant

Welcome to the Financial AI Assistant. This chatbot answers questions about company financials using data from the database.

## Capabilities

- Look up financial metrics for specific companies and years
- Calculate growth rates between periods
- Compute profit margins
- Compare companies across metrics
- Analyze trends over time

## Available Data

- **Companies**: 10 companies (Alpha Corp, Beta Inc, etc.)
- **Years**: 2019-2023
- **Metrics**: Revenue, Net Income, Total Assets, Total Equity

## Example Questions

- "What was Alpha Corp's revenue in 2022?"
- "Which company had the highest net income in 2023?"
- "How did Delta PLC's revenue grow between 2019 and 2023?"
- "Compare net margins of Beta Inc and Gamma Ltd"

All answers are based on actual database data, not AI-generated estimates.
